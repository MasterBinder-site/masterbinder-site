Privacy Policy

MasterBinder TCG does not require an account or login.

The app stores collection data locally on the user's device, including collection progress, wishlist cards, chase cards, and backup data.

MasterBinder TCG does not sell personal data.

The app may use Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect certain data for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention according to Google's own privacy policies.

If you contact support by email, your email address and message will only be used to respond to your request.
